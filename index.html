Vocabulary, Phrases, and Grammar Review

單字列表

  • 1. authentic (adj.)：道地的、真正的

    SYN: real, genuine

    Example: An Indian friend gave me this **authentic** curry recipe and I like it very much.

  • 2. exchange (n./v.)：交換

    vt. SYN: switch

    Example: The soccer players **exchanged** their T-shirts after the game.

  • 3. program (n.)：計畫、程式

    Example: Stanley joined a summer **program** which offered free English lessons.

  • 15. arrange (vt.)：安排

    Example: Joyce **arranged** a party for her daughter's birthday.

片語列表

  • 1. take part in：參加

    Example: Susan **took part in** the talent show.

  • 2. open one's eyes：令人大開眼界

    Example: Houdini's performance had **opened the boy's eyes**.

  • 3. take place：舉行、發生

    Example: Fuji Rock Festival **takes place** every year.

文法重點：形容詞子句 (Relative Clauses)

形容詞子句用於修飾名詞，通常由關係代名詞 (who, whom, which, that) 或關係副詞 (when, where, why) 引導。

重點： 當關係代名詞在子句中作受詞時，可以省略 (如：`that` 或 `which`)。

Example: This is the book (which/that) I bought yesterday.
